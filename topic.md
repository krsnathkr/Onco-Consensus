# Title: Onco-Consensus: Multi-Agent Diagnostic Reasoning for Breast Cancer Pathology and Analytics Platform

# Objective:
Design and implement a Multi-Agent System (MAS) that leverages Generative AI to perform diagnostic reasoning on breast cancer pathology data. The system must utilize a "consensus" mechanism where multiple AI agents—powered by different Large Language Models (LLMs)—analyze the data, debate findings, and converge on a diagnostic conclusion.


# Technical Environment:

You are required to use Cursor or Claude Code as your primary development environment. Your multi-agent architecture must integrate at least three of the following platforms/models to simulate diverse expert opinions:

- Anthropic Claude

- Google Gemini

- OpenAI ChatGPT

- xAI Grok

- Meta Llama (via LlamaIndex or similar)

Format: A fully functional Jupyter Notebook (.ipynb) or a repository link (GitHub/GitLab).
